WHERE IT ALL BEGAN From UT to AZ...

I was born in Utah. My family moved to Arizona when my dad had finished school. I grew up with eight younger siblings and absolutely loved it! This is also when I met my husband, Garrett. We ended up dating our last two years of high school.

The Mission(s)

Garrett had been on his mission in Brazil for seven months by the time I left to serve in the Peru Huancayo Mission in February of 2020. Little did we know, but COVID was coming, and after being sent home, I was reassigned to the Washington Yakima Mission. (Fun fact: Garrett and I were both reassigned to Washington! That was before our stake president let them know we were dating, however.) Garrett ended up finishing his mission in Colorado.

...and Back Again

Before I returned from my mission, my family moved back to Utah. When I got home, Garrett moved to Utah to be close while I started school. We were married October 16th of 2021, and Garrett worked the following year. Now we are living in Orem and both attending BYU full time.
